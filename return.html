RETURNS

1. Returns and Exchanges

We want you to love your Girl Almighty purchases. If you're not entirely satisfied with your order, we're here to help. We offer returns and exchanges within 7 days from the date of delivery.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or exchange, the item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging with all tags and labels intact.

3. How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer support team at girlalmighty@gmail.com to request a Return Authorization number.
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all accessories and documents.
  • Clearly write the RA number on the outside of the package.
  • Ship the package to the address provided by our customer support team.
4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original method of payment within 7 business days.

5. Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange your item for a different size or style, please indicate this when requesting your RA number. Exchanges are subject to availability. If the requested item is unavailable, we will process a refund for the original item.

6. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a mistake on our part. In case of an exchange, we will cover the shipping cost of the replacement item.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will provide instructions for returning the item, and once we confirm the damage or defect, we will offer a full refund or exchange, including return shipping costs.

8. Final Sale Items

Certain items, are marked as final sale and are not eligible for return or exchange.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please contact our customer support team at girlalmighty@gmail.com.
